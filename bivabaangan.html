The location of the tower is on the most prominent wide ring road of bubaneswar gohira to nandankanan to cuttack to puri new NH-203 to NH- 5 as per BDA planning.

Building Specification

  • Structure: Earth quake resistance R.C.C. frame structure with in-filled work above foundation.
  • Flooring: All floors of Vitrified tiles with 4'' Dado inside the flats. Marble/Kota flooring in Corridors/common areas.
  • Kitchen:Vitrified tiles, flooring, granite platform with stainless steel sink, glazed wall tiles up to 3' height over platform, one water purifier point and one exhaust fan point.
  • Toilets:Anti-skid tiles on floor and wall tiles up to 7 ft. from floor. Ceramic ware and C.P Fittings of parry ware/marc or equivalent make.
  • Doors:Front Door of teaks wood and all interior doors are flush doors with Sal Wood Frame.
  • Windows:Powder coated Aluminum shutter windows with glass panel and safety grill.
  • Electrical:Concealed wiring network with modular switches of good quality
  • Wall Finish:Putty with 2 coats of white primer on internal walls. Combination of texture and weather resistant paint on outer walls

Building Features

  • Fully Equipped Gym.Jogging Track.
  • Pool table.
  • Card and Society Room.
  • 6 Passenger Automatic (OTIS) Lift.
  • 100% Power Backup for Common & Residential Areas Including Lift
  • 24hrs Water Supply.
