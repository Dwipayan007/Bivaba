Company EHS Policy

BIVAB DEVELOPERS believes in developing and constructing aesthetically designed, functionally efficient residential and commercial complexes of international quality, offering "more value for money". We believe our buildings should reflect engineering excellence with a view to providing complete customer satisfaction. It is our intention that the quality of our products and services should result in complete value for our clients, as well as foster continuous demand for our products. While rendering our construction services, we shall strive to make Environmental, Health and Safety (EHS) matters as an integral part of our business.

As a responsible corporate citizen and employer, Bivab Developers commits itself to :

  • Ensure Health and Safety of all its employees.

Founder

Er. Binay Krishna Das – Profile

Er. Binay Krishna Das is the Chairman and Managing Director of Bivab Developers Private Ltd, one of Odisha's fastest growing property development companies. Under his steward leadership, the company has completed 17 glorious years and has built world class residential properties and Office buildings. A business leader with a clear vision for the industry. Mr. Das was the President of the Odisha chapter of the Confederation of Real Estate Developers' Associations of India (CREDAI).

At the core of Mr. Das endeavour is his credo of PQRST (Punctuality, Quality, Reliability, Speed and Transparency). This value framework places the customer at the heart of every project and indeed every activity. Through this, Mr. Das has succeeded in infusing a degree of professionalism hitherto unseen in the realty sector. Mr. Das’s new age thinking has enabled Bivab Developers to be the leaders in adopting green technology and environmentally sustainable building practices. Mr. Das has demonstrated the power of his vision and the strength of his values. No wonder he stands as a colossal figure in industry circles, inspiring others with his quest for newer and greater heights
